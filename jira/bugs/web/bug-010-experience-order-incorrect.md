# Bug 010 – Candidate experience not sorted from oldest to newest

---

## 📌 Summary
Candidate experience entries are not sorted from oldest to newest as expected.

---

## 🧪 Environment
- Application: Huntd (Web)
- Browser: Chrome 145.0.7632.76

---

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the main page
2. Navigate to the Candidates page
3. Open any candidate profile
4. Go to the "Experience" section

---

## ❌ Actual Result
Experience entries are displayed in incorrect order (not sorted from oldest to newest).

---

## ✅ Expected Result
Experience entries should be sorted from oldest to newest.

---

## ⚠️ Severity
Medium

---

## 🚨 Priority
Medium
