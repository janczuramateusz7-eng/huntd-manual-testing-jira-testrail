# Bug 007 – Company name displayed inconsistently across pages

---

## 📌 Summary
Company name is displayed inconsistently across different pages (e.g., "GlobalID", "Globalid", "GlobaliD").

---

## 🧪 Environment
- Application: Huntd (Web)
- Browser: Chrome 145.0.7632.76

---

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the main page
2. Scroll down to the footer section
3. Click on the "GlobalID" company logo
4. Observe the company name on different pages

---

## ❌ Actual Result
Company name is displayed inconsistently across pages (different capitalization formats).

---

## ✅ Expected Result
Company name should be displayed consistently across all pages.
